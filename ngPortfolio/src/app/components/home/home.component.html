About Me

I started off in retail sales and eventually started a book editing business. While I love editing, I always felt like it wasn't enough for me, so I decided to take a full-stack Java development bootcamp that was available to me through a veteran program and absolutely fell in love. I'd love to grow in this industry, continue to learn as much as possible along the way, and hopefully manage a team someday.

When I'm not working on personal projects, you can find me reading or editing books, playing video games, or doing DIY home projects. In my free time I love to shop and find great food to eat because life isn't worth living if you aren't eating delicious food.
